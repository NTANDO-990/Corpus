About UZ Translator Corpus

The UZ Translator Corpus is a project dedicated to providing accurate and efficient translations between various indigenous languages. Our goal is to bridge communication gaps and promote the use of these languages in everyday life.

We utilized advanced machine learning algorithms and a comprehensive database of linguistic resources to ensure high-quality translations. Whether you're a student, professional, or simply someone interested in South African indigenous languages, our app is designed to meet your translation needs.

Our translator offers translation among the most popular languages one can use to communicate with people of different cultures, these languages include isiZulu, isiSwati, and Xhosa. Our Corpus features audio translation to further help with YOU learning the proper pronounciation.
